About Us

We are a multidisciplinary team of health psychologists, public health experts and behavioural scientists with a passion for applying evidence to improve practice. We take pride in working closely with our clients and priority populations.

This website is created for educational purposes. The Behaviour Bureau as depicted on this website is a fictional organisation, part of an authentic professional task in the Health Promotion programme at Maastricht University.
